Jalapeño Steak Recipe

Description

This grilled jalapeño steak has a spicy and delicious kick. Marinate this steak in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours before grilling.

Ingredients

  • 4 jalapeno peppers, stemmed
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons cracked black pepper
  • 1 ½ pounds top sirloin steak
